There are rare moments in history when a new technology doesn't just improve how we work — it fundamentally redefines what work means. The steam engine ended the era of manual labor as the primary source of economic power. The internet collapsed the cost of information to near zero. Artificial intelligence, and specifically the large language model revolution we are living through right now, is doing something arguably more profound: it is democratizing *cognitive leverage*.

For the first time in history, every professional at every level of an organization has access to something that used to be reserved for the most elite: an infinitely patient, extraordinarily well-read thinking partner available around the clock, at zero marginal cost, with expertise spanning law, finance, medicine, technology, communication, data analysis, and virtually every other domain of human knowledge. That thinking partner is already sitting inside your Microsoft 365 account. It is called Copilot.

This book is your master class for understanding it, using it, and — most importantly — thinking with it.

## Why Helm Bank? Why Now?

Helm Bank USA is not just a bank. It is a story of resilience, vision, and purpose that began in 1989, when its founder turned the challenge of the Savings & Loan Crisis into an opportunity to build something genuinely different: a financial bridge between the United States and the world. Headquartered at 999 Brickell Avenue in Miami — the gateway to Latin America — Helm Bank has spent more than three decades building meaningful financial connections for multinational companies and foreign nationals pursuing their dreams of investment, growth, and opportunity in the American marketplace.

Under the leadership of President and CEO **Mark Anthony Crisp**, supported by CFO **Alejandro Safie** and Chief Lending Officer **Mari Blanco**, Helm Bank has grown into Miami's premier international gateway bank — with over **\$1.1 billion in assets**, a team of approximately **158 professionals**, and a presence stretching from Miami's Brickell financial district to Orlando, Florida, and a representative office in **Bogotá, Colombia**.

That reach is not accidental. It reflects a culture built on four core values: **Empathy, Curiosity, Creativity, and Passion** — and a commitment to serving clients where they are, in their language, across borders and time zones. Helm Bank is privately held, boutique by design, and deeply relationship-driven: 158 employees means everyone knows everyone, and every client interaction matters. The bank's proprietary **HelmInOne** digital platform — available on iOS and Android with multi-currency wire transfers, biometric authentication, and international treasury formats — reflects an institution that takes modernization seriously without losing the personal touch that defines it.

Organizations with this clarity of mission don't rest on past success. They look ahead. They identify what's coming before it arrives. And what is coming — what is already here — is the AI transformation of financial services.

The question is not whether AI will reshape banking. It already has. The question is whether the professionals inside Helm Bank will shape *how* that transformation happens, or whether they will watch from the sidelines while the opportunity passes them by.

This master class says: you will not watch. You will lead.

## How to Use This Book

This is a **hands-on master class**. Every chapter includes "Try This" exercises that take you directly into your Microsoft 365 environment and have you interact with Copilot in real time. The exercises are not optional homework. They are the actual learning. Reading about a bicycle and riding a bicycle are entirely different experiences.

You will need:
- A Microsoft 365 account with Copilot enabled (check with your IT administrator)
- Access to [m365.cloud.microsoft](https://m365.cloud.microsoft) — this is your front door to Enterprise Copilot
- About 15 minutes of focused time per chapter for the exercises

No prior technical knowledge is required. If you can write an email, you can learn to use AI.

## A Note on Voice

This book will speak to you directly. We will use "you" throughout because this transformation is personal. What AI does for your career, your productivity, and your value inside Helm Bank is determined by what *you* choose to do with it. The tools are neutral. The results are up to you.

One more thing before we begin. There is a quiet revolution happening in how people interact with AI that we want to put in front of you early. Tools like **Wispr Flow** and **Super Whisper** allow you to speak to your computer — to your AI — the same way you would speak to a colleague. Instead of typing out your prompts, you simply *talk*. Your words are transcribed and sent to Copilot in real time. People who adopt voice-first AI interaction report that their outputs are dramatically better: more natural, more nuanced, more complete. The reason is simple — we speak far faster than we type, and we explain things more naturally in speech than in writing.

Before this program is over, we want you to try it. You may never go back.
